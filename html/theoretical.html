CERTIFICATES

Who doesn't like certificates? I think they are worth the investment for two main reasons:

(1) Being certified allows me to prove how to implement prevention techniques that can make it harder for an attacker to access a network or application.

(2) I learn to work successfully in a technical environment and am comfortable with different systems and networks related to cybersecurity.

This also shows that I have the initiative and determination to get things done.
